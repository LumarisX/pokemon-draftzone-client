League Rules

The Rules are not finalized, once finalized an announcement will be made in the DraftZone discord server.

Draft

  • The draft order is randomized and follows a snake format (1-16, 16-1, etc.).
  • Each coach must draft between 10 and 12 Pokémon with a 120-point budget.
  • A coach can end the draft after 10 picks without spending the full budget.
  • Tera Captains are chosen after a 24-hour grace period following the draft.
  • Drafting will start on TBD with a 8-hour timer per pick. The timer will be lowered as necessary as the draft progresses with notice.
  • Coaches can leave picks with a tournament organizer or another participant behind them in the order.
  • If a pick is skipped, the coach can make it later, but the timer for future picks will be halved each time a skip occurs.

Terastalisation

  • Each team has 20 Tera Shards to allocate to Tera Captains.
  • Tera Captaincy costs the same number of Tera Shards as the drafted point value of the Pokémon.
  • A Tera Captain can have three Tera Types: one matching their original type and two additional types, including Stellar.
  • Teams may have up to 3 Tera Captains within the 20-point limit.
  • Only Pokémon with a value of 12 points or less can be Tera Captains.
  • The following Pokémon cannot be Tera Captains: Shedinja, Regieleki, Terapagos, Espathra, Cloyster, Polteageist.

Megas

  • Megas and regular forms are drafted separately with corresponding point values.
  • If a Mega form is drafted, its held item must be its Mega Stone, although usage in battle is optional. Opponents will always know the item.
  • If a regular form is drafted, it cannot Mega Evolve in battle.

Free Agency and Trades

  • Teams have up to 5 Free Agency transactions through the end of Week 6.
  • A trade transaction is used for every pokemon you recieve from the draft pool.
  • Teams have up to 5 inter-team trades through the end of Week 6.
  • A 24-hour grace period allows for unlimited Free Agency and trades before selecting Tera Captains.
  • Reassigning Tera Captaincy within the team does consume a transaction.
  • Tera types may only be changed once per season, using one of the 7 transactions.
  • Transactions take effect the following week.

Discord and Scheduling

  • All coaches must be in the DraftZone server for the duration of the tournament.
  • All match scheduling must be made in the # scheduling channel in the DraftZone server.
  • If a game isn't played in the regular week, it must be scheduled for the 2-day extension period.
  • Extensions or rescheduling may be allowed in cases of serious, sudden issues and can be requested in # extension-request.
  • Coaches with better communication may be awarded wins in case of unplayed games.

Battles

  • Each week, teams have a best-of-1 match in National Dex Draft Singles format. Extensions allow up to two additional days.
  • Forfeits prior to the match count as a 2-0 outcome. Mid-match forfeits are prohibited.
  • All Pokémon will be Level 100.
  • No ghosting or coaching during battles.
  • Sharing a player’s team or build with their opponent is prohibited and will result in penalties, including voided games and bans.
  • Each team must bring 6 Pokémon to every battle.
  • All battles will be on Showdown, following Gen9 NatDex Draft rules.

League Table and Playoffs

  • Each team will play 8 matches. The schedule determines opponents.
  • Standings are determined in the following order: game wins, game differential, head-to-head, strength of schedule, coin flip.
  • Playoffs involve the top 8 teams, following a standard seeding bracket.
  • Playoff scheduling follows a 7+2-day cycle for 3 weeks total.

Battle Clauses & Bans

  • Smogon Clauses: Sleep Clause, Evasion Clause, OHKO Clause, and Endless Battle Clause are in effect.
  • Banned abilities: Moody, Sand Veil, Snow Cloak.
  • Banned items: Bright Powder, Lax Incense, King's Rock, Focus Band, Razor Fang, Quick Claw.
  • Banned moves: Hidden Power, Shed Tail, Last Respects, Revival Blessing, Z Moves.
  • Games ending on a timer will be counted as a loss for the team that went to time.
  • Baton Pass cannot be used to pass stats or Substitute unless granted by the opponent.
  • Species Clause: Teams cannot draft multiple Pokémon with the same Pokédex number.
