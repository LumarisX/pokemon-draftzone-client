About Pokemon DraftZone

Welcome to Pokemon DraftZone, your ultimate resource for Pokemon draft leagues! Our goal is to provide you with clear and informative data about your draft and your opponents, empowering you to strategize and excel in your matches.

Drafts

View and manage your drafts effortlessly with DraftZone. Whether you're in the midst of a current draft or revisiting archived ones, our platform provides a user-friendly interface to keep track of your teams and progress.

  • Current and Archived Drafts: Access your current draft as well as archived drafts with ease. Whether you're preparing for an upcoming match or analyzing past performances, DraftZone ensures all your draft data is readily available and organized.
  • Team Statistics: Identify standout performers and areas for improvement within your draft lineup using the tracked statistics. Track MVPs who consistently deliver strong performances and pinpoint underperforming team members who may require adjustments.
  • Support for Multiple Generations and Formats: From classic battles to the latest VGC format, DraftZone accommodates a variety of preferences. With support for multiple generations, including National Dex formats, players can enjoy drafting teams tailored to their preferred playstyle, whether it's Singles or Doubles.

Track Opponents

Stay informed about your opponents' draft selections week by week. From monitoring draft selections to recording match results, DraftZone helps you strategize effectively for upcoming matchups.

  • Match Overview: Quickly assess the status of upcoming matches and review past matchups at a glance. Easily navigate through scheduled games and view detailed summaries of matches that have already taken place, including scores, teams, and outcomes.
  • Score and Match Results Recording: Record scores, kills, deaths, and pastes to track your performance in the league accurately.
  • Scheduled Playing Time (Beta): Set designated match times, convert times across timezones, and receive reminders to ensure timely and organized gameplay. Never miss a match again with DraftZone's convenient scheduling tools.

Prepare for Matches

In the draft preparation page, we provide you with an array of tools and insights to help you strategize effectively for your upcoming matches.

  • Overview: Get a quick snapshot of your team's composition compared to your opponent's. Understand the overall balance of power and identify potential areas for improvement or exploitation.
  • Summary: Dive deeper into the specifics with detailed analyses of base stats, typing, and abilities of each Pokemon on both teams. Discover the greatest strengths and weaknesses of each lineup to refine your strategy.
  • Speed Chart: Explore how different factors such as natures, held items like Choice Scarf, or effects like Tailwind influence Pokemon's speed. Adjust your final speed stats accordingly to gain a crucial advantage in battle.
  • Defensive Type Chart: Understand teams' defensive capabilities and identify potential vulnerabilities to exploit. Toggle Pokemon on and off to assess changes in type coverage dynamically.
  • Learned Moves: Quickly identify important moves the teams has access to, such as setup moves, hazard control, or status-inducing moves. This information enables you to anticipate threats and plan your counter-strategies accordingly.
  • Move Coverage: Highlight which moves each Pokemon can use and identify any gaps in offensive coverage. Determine there are Pokemon that the selected moves can't deal hit effectively.
  • Share Your Matchup: Use the share link feature to invite teammates, coaches, or fellow players to review your matchup, even if they don't have a DraftZone account. Say goodbye to awkwardly screenshotting and start collaborating seamlessly.

Plan Your Draft

Start planning your drafts with ease. Our DraftZone Planner provides everything you need to organize your picks, review strategies, and prepare for your upcoming draft leagues.

  • Draft Planning: Pre-plan your picks before the draft begins. Create a draft board, rank Pokemon, and ensure you're prepared for each draft round.
  • Team Building: Experiment with different combinations of Pokemon to find the best lineup. Simulate draft scenarios and prepare contingency plans.
